# ProfitAly - Advanced Financial Analytics Platform

ProfitAly is a modern financial analytics platform that provides comprehensive market data, advanced AI-driven predictions, and expert analysis to help investors make informed decisions.

## Integrated Services

ProfitAly leverages several external APIs to provide rich financial data and insights:

### Yahoo Finance
- Real-time and historical stock price data  
- Company profiles and fundamental information  
- Financial statements and key metrics  
- Market news and trends  

### Perplexity AI
- AI-powered financial insights and recommendations  
- Stock sentiment analysis  
- Price predictions and market forecasts  
- Personalized investment advice  

### MarketBeat
- Analyst ratings and price targets  
- Insider trading activity  
- Earnings data and forecasts  
- Institutional ownership information  

### Focus Guru
- Advanced technical analysis  
- Social media sentiment tracking  
- AI-driven price predictions  
- Fundamental health assessment
